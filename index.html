About Me

The person
behind the words.

Hi, I'm Celine Parra — a writer based in Crown Point, IN, USA. I specialize in academic and informative articles with a particular focus on healthcare, health and wellness technology, and education.

My work has appeared in Klarity Health Library. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Purdue University Northwest. I also hold a Bachelor of Science in Health Studies from Purdue University and a Master of Health Informatics from Purdue University Global.

When I'm not studying or writing, you'll find me working at my university Writing Center, helping students from various degree programs with whatever writing work they need help with. And when I'm not doing any of that, I'm listening to music, learning languages, and spending time with my family.
